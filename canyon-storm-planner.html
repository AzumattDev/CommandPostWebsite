Game Rules — Canyon Storm
⏱️30-minute battle. Point lead at end wins.
🏠Base HP = 6,000. Destroyed after 10 hits.
⚡Power Tower gives RB a shield — hold at all costs.
🦠Viro Lab = 120 pts/sec. Highest value building — unlocks at 12:00.
🔒Serum Factories unlock at 5:00. Defense Systems at 8:00.
🚫Max 5 players per building. Teleport CD = 3 min base.
🔄Substitutes: switch in when a player runs out of troops.
📢Communicate! Call for help when any building is attacked.
