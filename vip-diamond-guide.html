F2P VIP strategy

Claim the free daily VIP chest (up to 200 points/day). Join a strong alliance — whale purchases give everyone VIP points through alliance gift chests. Spend diamonds on VIP points when you're close to a milestone level (3, 6, 7, 8). The 30-day activation is always the best diamond-to-time ratio.

Diamond spending priority (F2P & low spender)

Diamonds are scarce. This is the strict order of where they should go:

  1. VIP levels → reach VIP 6
    The 2nd permanent builder is the single biggest progression unlock for diamonds. After VIP 6, continue toward VIP 8 for Agent Shirley and the extra march.
    1 diamond = 1 VIP point
  2. VIP Store: Stamina (every Monday)
    Buy all 5× Stamina (50 each) = 300 diamonds/week. This is the best value in the entire shop. Stamina fuels campaign progress, hero XP farming, and radar missions. Buy this without fail.
    300 💎/week — non-negotiable
  3. VIP Store: 8h & 3h Speedups
    Best diamond-to-time ratio for acceleration. Use them to hit Arms Race milestones or finish critical HQ levels. Only buy when you have an active project that needs them.
    Buy only when needed
  4. Shields (before Kill Events)
    8h, 12h, or 24h shields before you go offline during Kill Events or Saturday VS combat. Losing troops costs far more than a shield.
    Situational — don't skip
  5. Teleports
    Move closer to your Alliance Hive. Essential for coordination, alliance helps, and protection. Buy as needed.
    Situational
  6. Drone Components (VIP Store)
    Drones are one of the strongest force multipliers. If you have surplus diamonds after the above, drone components from the VIP Store are a great long-term investment.
    Surplus diamonds only
  7. VIP Store: Skill Medals (Season 2+)
    Skill medals become a major bottleneck in Season 2+. Only buy if you have 5,000+ diamond surplus and your main hero's skills are lagging behind.
    4 💎 each — only when surplus

Diamond spending traps

Never use diamonds to speed up timers directly — the exchange rate is terrible. Buy speedups from the VIP Store instead. Never buy random pop-up resource packs. Never spend diamonds on stamina refills beyond the VIP Store's weekly 5 purchases. The VIP Store resets every Monday — don't let it go to waste.

Diamond income sources (F2P)

Daily tasks (up to 30 diamonds), 3v3 Brawl thumbs up (10 diamonds/day), alliance gift chests from whale purchases, event rewards, campaign progression, and code redemptions. In a strong alliance, you can earn thousands of free diamonds per week just from alliance gifts.
